The Car Security System

What We Do -

Protect your car

Combining the features of the electrically switchable glass (ESG) with Facial Recognition Software (using OpenCV) and Raspberry pi to enable windshield blackening (turning Opaque) preventing anyone from inside to see whereabouts outside the car. This ensures that even if the car’s external security measures are compromised, the car cannot be driven away and is secured. Our product is kept separate from the car’s main power supply to ensure the jumpstart to the car doesn’t fire electrically switchable glass (ESG). Once an unrecognized face is encountered windshield turns opaque and an image of that person is sent to the car owner
